# Privacy Policy for Sama

**Effective Date: February 26, 2026**

Sama ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

### 1. No Data Collection
Sama is designed to work completely offline. We do **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including your name, email address, or contact details.

### 2. Location Services
Sama requires access to your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. 
- This calculation happens **locally** on your device.
- Your location data is **never** uploaded to any server.
- Your location data is **never** shared with third parties.

### 3. Third-Party Services
Sama does not use any third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks.
