# Sama Support

Thank you for using **Sama - Prayer, Quran & Qibla**. We are dedicated to providing a serene and accurate spiritual experience.

### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Why are the prayer times slightly different from my local mosque?**
A: Please check the "Calculation Method" in the settings. Different regions use different conventions (e.g., Muslim World League vs. Umm al-Qura).

**Q: How do I get accurate Qibla direction?**
A: Ensure you are away from large metal objects or electronic devices that might interfere with the compass. Hold your phone flat.

**Q: Why am I not receiving notifications?**
A: Please ensure that Notifications are enabled for Sama in your iPhone's System Settings.
